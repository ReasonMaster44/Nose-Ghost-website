About

Born out of a drunken night out in Camden, Nose Ghost live and breathe the rock-n-roll dream. The four-piece indie outfit hailing from London have been moving from strength to strength since their debut in 2024. With sold out shows across the city and a full length album on the way, there’s no sign of stopping these lads, and they’re only just getting started…

Anthemic and impossible to ignore, their new single South is filled with undeniably catchy hooks, whether delivered through vocals, guitars or synths.

Work on “The Debrief”, their debut full length record is well underway. With the singles Anaconda Malt Liquor, South and Spiders giving but a small taste of what’s to come - wether it’s catchy indie anthems or loud, in-yer-face garage rock, the band is just as comfortable with distorted guitars as they are with acoustics and harmonicas. Nose Ghost are loud, unapologetic and crucially, themselves.
